Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions about Medicomp
to help you get the information you need. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to our support team
How accurate are the prices listed on Medicomp India?

We strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date information on medicine prices. However, prices may vary depending on the location and availability of the medicine

Can I purchase medicines directly from Medicomp India?

No, Medicomp India does not sell medicines directly. We provide information on the cost of medicines and where to find them, but you will need to purchase the medicines directly from the pharmacy.

What if I can't find the medicine I'm looking for on Medicomp India?

If you can't find the medicine you're looking for on our platform, please contact us and we'll do our best to help you find it.

Can I use Medicomp India on my mobile device?

Yes, our platform is mobile-friendly and can be accessed from any device with an internet connection.

How can I provide feedback or report an issue with Medicomp India?

We welcome your feedback and encourage you to contact us with any questions, comments, or concerns. You can reach us by email, phone, or through our contact form on the website.

Does Medicomp India provide information on prescription medicines only, or can I compare prices for over-the-counter medicines as well?

Medicomp India provides pricing information for both prescription and over-the-counter medicines. You can search for and compare prices for any medicine that is available in your area.
