Information

1 AL 5MG TABLET contains Levocetirizine which belongs to the group of medicines called Antihistamines. It is used to treat symptoms associated with allergic rhinitis (such as nasal congestion, itchy nose, repeated sneezing and red, watery and/or itchy eyes) and urticaria (allergic skin condition causing reddish raised patches on the skin with severe itching) in both adults and in children (aged 6 years or above).Take 1 AL 5MG TABLET as directed by your physician. It is preferably taken during the evenings or at bedtime with or without meals. Before taking 1 AL 5MG TABLET, inform your doctor if you have any liver or kidney problems. You must also inform your doctor if you have epilepsy or difficulty in passing urine.1 AL 5MG TABLET is not recommended for use in patients with severe kidney impairment. It should be used in pregnant or breastfeeding women only if it is considered necessary by the physician.1 AL 5MG TABLET may cause excessive drowsiness and tiredness. Therefore, restrict yourself from engaging into activities that requires complete concentration (such as driving or handling heavy tools or machines) as a precaution.Other most common side effects of taking 1 AL 5MG TABLET are dryness of mouth, headache and constipation. Consult your doctor if they trouble you.
 
Uses

  • Chronic Idiopathic Urticaria (severe skin rash with reddish raised patches and itching)
  • Allergic rhinitis (such as nasal congestion, itchy nose, repeated sneezing and red, watery, and itchy eyes)

    Direction To Use 6

    Take 1 AL 5MG TABLET as advised by your physician. It can be taken with or without meals.For Tablet, Capsule: Swallow the medicine with a glass of water. Do not crush or chew the medicine.For Dispersible Tablet: Disperse the medicine in the specified quantity of water as mentioned in the label. Consume the medicine immediately after reconstitution.For Mouth Dissolving Tablet, Orally Disintegrating Tablet: Place the medicine inside your mouth and allow it to dissolve completely. Do not swallow or chew the medicine.Your doctor will decide the correct dose and duration of therapy for you depending upon your age, body weight and disease condition. Continue to take this medicine for the stipulated duration prescribed by your doctor to achieve better results.
