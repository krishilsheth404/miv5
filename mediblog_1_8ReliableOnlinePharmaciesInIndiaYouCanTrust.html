8 Reliable Online Pharmacies You Can Trust

In recent times, the idea of visiting a local chemist for buying medicines has taken a backseat as more people are opting for the convenience and affordability offered by online pharmacies. With the increasing popularity of e-commerce in India, a number of online pharmacies have emerged in the market. But the question that arises is, how reliable are these online pharmacies? To help you with your search, we have compiled a list of 10 reliable online pharmacies in India that you can trust.





1) Apollo Pharmacy

  • Apollo Pharmacy is one of the largest and most trusted online pharmacies in India. It is a part of the Apollo Hospitals group, which is known for its world-class healthcare services.
  • With a network of over 3,500 stores across India, Apollo Pharmacy is known for its quality medicines and excellent customer service.
  • One of the key advantages of Apollo Pharmacy is that it offers a wide range of prescription drugs, OTC medicines, health and wellness products, and medical equipment. It also has an easy-to-use website and mobile app, making it convenient for customers to order medicines online.


    • 2) Netmeds


  • Netmeds is one of the leading online pharmacies in India with a wide range of prescription and over-the-counter medicines available for purchase.
  • The platform has become popular among customers due to its easy-to-use interface and timely delivery of medicines. Netmeds also offers a wide range of healthcare products, including wellness products, personal care products, and more.
  • Netmeds offers doorstep delivery of medicines within a few days of ordering. They also have an efficient customer care team that is available 24/7 to help customers with their queries and complaints.
  • If you are looking for a reliable online pharmacy in India, Netmeds is a great option.


    • 3) 1mg

  • 1mg is an online pharmacy that provides access to a wide range of medicines, wellness products, and healthcare services. The platform was launched in 2015 and has since grown to become one of the most trusted online pharmacies in India.
  • What sets 1mg apart from other online pharmacies is their commitment to customer service. The platform has a team of trained pharmacists who are available 24/7 to answer any questions or concerns customers may have.


    • 4) Medlife


  • Medlife is one of the top online pharmacies in India that offers a wide range of medicines and healthcare products at affordable prices. It is known for its fast delivery and genuine products.
  • Medlife provides customers with the convenience of ordering medicines from the comfort of their own home and also offers free consultation with certified doctors.
  • In addition to medicines, Medlife also offers a range of personal care and healthcare products including baby care, nutrition, medical equipment, and much more. Medlife's unique features include 24x7 customer service, easy payment options, and genuine discounts.
  • To ensure the authenticity of medicines, Medlife sources its products directly from licensed suppliers. The company also offers free sample collections and tests, allowing customers to easily order tests from the comfort of their home.


    • 5) Pharmeasy

  • Pharmeasy is one of the leading online pharmacies in India, offering a wide range of medicines, healthcare products, and medical equipment. With their user-friendly interface and fast delivery service, Pharmeasy has become a go-to destination for many Indian consumers who prefer to shop online.
  • One of the best things about Pharmeasy is their convenient subscription service, where customers can set up recurring orders for their regular medication. This ensures that they never run out of essential medication and do not have to worry about refilling their prescription every month.
  • Pharmeasy also offers discounts and deals on their products, making them an affordable option for customers who are looking for cost-effective solutions.
  • Their online platform also features an easy-to-use search bar that allows users to search for specific products or brands, ensuring that they can quickly find what they are looking for.
  • Overall, Pharmeasy is a trusted online pharmacy in India that offers a wide range of products, fast delivery, affordable pricing, and excellent customer service.


    • 6) Saveonmeds



  • Saveonmeds is another reliable online pharmacy that provides a wide range of medicines and healthcare products. The platform is known for offering authentic products from leading brands at discounted prices, which makes it a favorite among budget-conscious shoppers.
  • Saveonmeds has a user-friendly website and mobile app, which makes it easy for customers to browse products and place orders. They offer flexible payment options including cash on delivery and online payments.
  • The platform has gained a good reputation for its prompt delivery and efficient customer support. They also have a strict quality control process to ensure that all products are genuine and of high quality.


    • 7)PulsePlus

  • PulsePlus is an online pharmacy based in India that offers a wide range of prescription drugs and healthcare products.
  • Another aspect that sets PulsePlus apart from other online pharmacies is their excellent customer support. They have a team of trained professionals who are available round the clock to answer customer queries and provide assistance whenever needed.
  • Moreover, PulsePlus takes all necessary measures to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of their customers' information. They use secure payment gateways and employ advanced security protocols to protect customer data from any unauthorized access.


    • 8) Flipkart HealthPlus

  • If you are a frequent online shopper, you must have heard of Flipkart. This popular e-commerce platform also has a separate section dedicated to health and wellness called Flipkart HealthPlus.
  • Here, you can purchase not just medicines but also other healthcare essentials like diagnostic kits, healthcare devices, personal care products, and more.
  • Overall, Flipkart HealthPlus is a reliable online pharmacy that you can trust for all your healthcare needs. It offers a wide range of medicines and healthcare products at affordable prices, making it a popular choice among customers. So, if you're looking to buy medicines online, don't forget to check out Flipkart HealthPlus.
